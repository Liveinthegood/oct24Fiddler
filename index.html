A TRUE LOVE STORY FOR ALL THE AGES!

This is from the great movie called Fiddler on the Roof from 1971. Here, it tells a brief story of the wonderful relationship between the eldest daughter Tzeitel and the towns tailor whos name is Motel.

Play your favorite song in life. Have fun, be successful, but don't fall off your "roof" and hurt yourself.

Tradition is extremely important within this community as the father has many goals to follow out to ensure happiness and/or balance. One responsibility is to see that his daugthers are married off when age appropriate.

The town matchmaker Yente sets up Lazor Wolf, the rich town butcher, with Tzeitel the eldest daugther.

Tevye, who is Tzeitel's father, is overjoyed by this news and after entertaining internal debate, he agrees to this match as it will ensure his daugthers future as prosperous.

But love conquerors all as Model wins over Tevye, the father, for her hand...
